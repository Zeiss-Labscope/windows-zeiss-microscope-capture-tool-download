## Overview

**ZEISS Labscope** is an advanced software tailored for digital microscopes, providing an intuitive platform for image capturing, analysis, and presentation. Ideal for educational and professional environments, **Labscope** simplifies the process of controlling microscopes, capturing high-resolution images, and analyzing them with ease. It is an essential tool for students, teachers, and researchers who require high-quality microscope imaging.

With **Labscope**, you can manage live imaging sessions, measure specimens, annotate images, and share results, all while leveraging a simple and intuitive interface.
